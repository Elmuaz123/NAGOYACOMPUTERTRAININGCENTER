Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before visiting Nagoya Computer Center.

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are available on public holidays with reduced hours.

Do I need to make a reservation in advance?

No reservation is needed for standard workstations — simply walk in and we'll set you up. However, for private booths and all-day passes, we recommend booking ahead by calling or visiting us.

What types of documents can you print or design?

We handle all standard document types including CVs, academic assignments, business proposals, flyers, banners, and certificates. Both black-and-white and full-color printing are available.

Is your internet connection fast and reliable?

Yes. We operate on high-speed fiber broadband with dedicated bandwidth per workstation. This ensures a stable, lag-free experience whether you are streaming, doing research, or transferring large files.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students with a valid school ID enjoy a 15% discount on hourly internet sessions and document printing. Ask our staff at the counter when you arrive.

Can I save my work and come back later?

You can save your work to a USB drive, email it to yourself, or use any cloud storage like Google Drive or OneDrive during your session. We do not store files on our computers after sessions end.
